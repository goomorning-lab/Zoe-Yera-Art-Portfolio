Hello~ My name is Zoe! I'm a game artist based in Busan, South Korea.

Throughout my career, I have dabbled in character design, illustration, and Live2D model work, where I've had the opportunity to bring characters to life from first sketch to a fully rigged, animated model. My primary interests involve concept art, character design, illustration, and rigging expressive Live2D models for games and VTubers. Through applying these skills, I've had the pleasure of creating original characters, reference sheets, and animated models, ultimately providing me experience with every stage of bringing a character to life.

Outside of work, I spend a lot of time playing RPGs like Kingdom Hearts and Final Fantasy XII. The imaginative worlds, strong visual identities, and memorable casts of these games have shaped my taste as an artist for years. They're a constant reminder of the kind of experiences that made me fall in love with games in the first place, and they continue to influence how I approach character design, color, atmosphere, and storytelling in my own work.

What I do

Art and animation, together.

Illustration & concept art
I create finished illustrations and concept art — key art, splash pieces, and expressions — balancing composition, color, and mood to fit a project's world.
Live2D modeling & rigging
I prepare art in layered parts and rig expressive Live2D models with smooth deformation and physics, ready for games and VTuber streaming.
Game art & assets
I produce game-ready art — sprites, UI, and in-game assets — keeping a consistent visual style across a project from first draft to final export.
